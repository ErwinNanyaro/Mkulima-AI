Who We Are

The Green Neighborhood is a passionate organization committed to combating climate change in line with SDG 13: “Take urgent action to combat climate change and its impacts.” Guided by our vision of creating a world where people and nature thrive together, we empower communities through education, capacity building, and hands-on initiatives like workshops, hiking events, and training.
