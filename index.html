Urban Projects

On January 25th, 2025, The Green Neighborhood conducted an environmental awareness campaign in Dar es Salaam, combining education on sustainable waste management with a hands-on clean-up initiative to empower the community, foster environmental stewardship, and promote healthier, greener urban spaces.

Demonstrating our commitment to environmental stewardship, The Green Neighborhood actively engaged in a hands-on clean-up initiative in Dar es Salaam, transforming awareness into action by restoring urban spaces and inspiring the community to embrace sustainable practices.

Rural Projects

Uluguru Mountain, a vital water source for Morogoro, is home to our Sustainable Strawberry Pathway where conservation, eco-friendly farming, and community empowerment thrive.

Through the Sustainable Strawberry Pathway, The Green Neighborhood empowers Uluguru Mountain farmers with eco-friendly practices, social capital, and agricultural innovation for a sustainable future.

Seated against the breathtaking backdrop of Uluguru Mountain, we engage with local elders to champion the protection of vital water resources, safeguarding the lifeline of Morogoro and ensuring future generations inherit a landscape as rich and resilient as its people.
