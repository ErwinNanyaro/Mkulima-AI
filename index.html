Welcome to Green Neighborhood

Our mission is to create sustainable futures for all.

Who We Are

Vision

To create a world where people and nature thrive together.

Mission

To explore, enjoy, and protect the wild places of the earth; to practice and promote the responsible use of the earth’s ecosystems and resources; to educate and enlist humanity to protect and restore the quality of the natural and human environment; and to use all lawful means to carry out these objectives.

Our Values

  • Sustainability – We are dedicated to promoting practices that ensure the long-term health of our planet and its ecosystems.
  • Accountability – We uphold transparency and responsibility in all our actions, ensuring trust and integrity in our efforts.
  • Collaboration – We believe in the power of partnerships, working with local and global stakeholders to achieve impactful and sustainable outcomes.

Founder Stories

Erwin Nanyaro

Hailing from Tanzania, Erwin Nanyaro is a dedicated climate advocate with a passion for creating sustainable futures. Their journey into climate action began with a transformative experience working on the landmark CLIFT project, a collaborative initiative by Mzumbe University and the University of Copenhagen. This project focused on smart climate agriculture and involved extensive data collection across Tanzania’s Iringa, Morogoro, and Arusha regions. While engaging with smallholder farmers, Erwin Nanyaro witnessed firsthand the devastating vulnerabilities they face due to climate impacts. The stark contrast between urban and rural resilience levels, shaped by unequal infrastructure priorities, ignited a deep commitment to champion climate justice. Inspired by the resilience of these communities, Erwin Nanyaro founded The Green Neighborhood to bridge these gaps and create tangible solutions for both urban and rural populations. As the founder of The Green Neighborhood, Erwin Nanyaro leads a passionate team dedicated to empowering communities through education, capacity building, and actionable climate initiatives. Under their leadership, the organization has become a beacon of hope, fostering collaboration and inspiring individuals to take urgent action against climate change. With a vision of creating a world where people and nature thrive together, Erwin Nanyaro has turned their academic expertise and field experience into a powerful platform for change. Their work continues to inspire individuals and organizations worldwide to join hands in building a sustainable and inclusive future. ...

Doreen D. Mosha

Doreen D. Mosha is a passionate advocate for environmental sustainability and a dedicated climate action leader. With a profound understanding of the challenges posed by climate change, Doreen has committed herself to creating practical solutions that address its impacts within her community and beyond. As the co-founder of the Green Neighborhood Organization, Doreen strives to inspire individuals and communities to take responsibility for protecting our planet. Her work focuses on raising awareness, promoting sustainable practices, and empowering people with the knowledge and tools to combat climate change effectively. Driven by the belief that people and nature can thrive together, she is devoted to fostering harmony between humanity and the environment. Her unwavering commitment is rooted in the conviction that every action, no matter how small, can create a ripple effect toward a healthier, more sustainable future. Through the Green Neighborhood Organization, Doreen aims to build a movement where everyone is a steward of the earth, contributing to a world where current and future generations can live and prosper in balance with nature. ...
